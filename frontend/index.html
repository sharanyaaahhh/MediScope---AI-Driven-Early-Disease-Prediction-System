How MediScope Works

MediScope connects patients and doctors around a shared AI risk score. Patients enter their vital signs, our model estimates their current health risk, and doctors get a clear view of who needs attention first.

1

Create your account

Patients and doctors register with basic information and a secure password. All credentials are stored using strong hashing, never in plain text.

2

Enter real-time vitals

Patients log in and enter blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, oxygen saturation, medication adherence, and symptom severity through a guided form.

3

AI risk prediction

A trained machine‑learning model analyzes the vitals, produces a risk level (low / high), confidence score, and suggests an appropriate specialist.

4

Doctor dashboard & follow-up

Doctors see all patients in a dashboard, sorted by risk, with recent assessments and suggested actions so they can prioritize who to contact or schedule next.

About MediScope

MediScope is a prototype clinical decision‑support tool built with Flask, SQLite and a machine‑learning model trained on structured health data. The goal is to help healthcare teams spot high‑risk patients early using objective vital‑sign measurements, not to replace doctors.

Patients get an easy‑to‑understand summary of their current risk level and a suggested specialist. Doctors see a dashboard of all registered patients with recent assessments, so they can quickly identify who may need a follow‑up call, medication adjustment or in‑person visit.

This project focuses on secure authentication, clear UX for non‑technical users, and transparent AI feedback to support safer real‑world deployment in the future.

  • Doctors see per‑patient history to support longitudinal care.
